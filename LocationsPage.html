Arches National Park

Arches National Park

Arches National Park, located in Utah, is another example of the marvels of nature. The park is filled with naturally formed arches, natural bridges, balanced rocks and many other rock formations. These are all effects of various water movements and waterbeds that start all the way back when the dinosaurs still roamed the Earth. Click for more information!

Monument Valley

Monument Valley, located on the Utah-Arizona border, is another area on the Colorado Plateau which is characterized by various rock formations, mainly by clusters of sandstone buttes. Once again these formations were created by long gone water movements. The formations in this area can show various different colours depending form the content of the minerals in the soil.
